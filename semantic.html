Argentina National Football Team

The Argentina national football team represents Argentina in international men’s soccer and is governed by the Argentine Football Association (AFA). Recognized as one of the most successful teams in world football, Argentina is celebrated for its technical style and for producing iconic players such as Diego Maradona and Lionel Messi.

Brazil National Football Team

The Brazil National Football Team represents Brazil in international men’s soccer and is governed by the Brazilian Football Confederation (CBF). Renowned for its creative, attacking style and technical brilliance, Brazil is the most successful team in FIFA World Cup history and a symbol of footballing excellence worldwide.

Portugal national football team

The Portugal national football team represents Portugal in men’s international football and is governed by the Federação Portuguesa de Futebol (FPF). A UEFA and FIFA member since 1923, the team is one of Europe’s most consistent powers, recognized for its technical flair and global stars such as Eusébio, Luís Figo, and Cristiano Ronaldo.

France national football team

The France national football team represents France in international men’s soccer and is governed by the French Football Federation. Known as Les Bleus (“The Blues”), it is one of the most successful national teams in the world, recognized for its technical style and historical depth in global football competitions.

England national football team

The England national football team represents England in men’s international football and is governed by The Football Association (FA). Competing under FIFA and UEFA, the team is one of the sport’s oldest and most historically influential sides, known as “The Three Lions.”

Germany National Football Team

The Germany national football team represents Germany in international men’s soccer competitions and is governed by the German Football Association. Known for its consistency and tactical excellence, the team is one of the most successful in world football, with multiple FIFA World Cup and UEFA European Championship titles.

Spain National Football Team

The Spain national football team represents Spain in international men’s soccer competitions and is governed by the Royal Spanish Football Federation. Known as “La Roja” (“The Red”), it is one of the most successful teams in world football, celebrated for its technical style and historic victories in the 21st century.

The FIFA World Cup Trophy is the most prestigious award in international football. Introduced in 1974, it is made of 18-carat gold and stands about 36.8 cm tall, weighing 6.1 kg. The trophy depicts two human figures holding up the Earth, symbolizing global unity through sport. It is awarded to the winning national team every four years. In the 2022 FIFA World Cup, FIFA distributed a record prize pool of $440 million, with the champions, Argentina national football team, receiving $42 million. Prize money is shared among teams based on their tournament performance.
